Our Services

Diagnostic Pathology

We provide comprehensive diagnostic pathology services, including histopathology, cytopathology, and hematopathology. Our expert pathologists ensure accurate diagnosis to aid in patient treatment plans.

Clinical Laboratory Testing

Our clinical laboratory offers a wide range of tests including blood tests, urinalysis, and microbiology cultures. We use advanced technology to deliver precise and reliable results.

Molecular Diagnostics

We offer molecular diagnostic services to detect and monitor genetic disorders, infectious diseases, and cancers. Our cutting-edge techniques include PCR, sequencing, and genotyping.

Immunohistochemistry

Our immunohistochemistry services help identify specific antigens in cells. This technique is crucial for diagnosing various types of cancers and understanding their behavior.

Consultation Services

We provide expert consultation services for complex cases. Our pathologists work closely with referring physicians to offer second opinions and detailed diagnostic insights.

Patient Support

We are committed to supporting our patients throughout their diagnostic journey. Our team is available to answer questions and provide information about test results and procedures.
